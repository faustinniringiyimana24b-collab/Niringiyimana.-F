Our Success and Achievemets Through Faustin's Education Hub Support

"My name is Jean MUNGUIKO and I am currently a final year student in National University of Rwanda (UR). Before being admitted here in the university I spent two years in camp looking for scholarship but it was hard to find. After knowing Faustin's Education Hub, they helped me as much as they could and I immediately got scholarship. Faustin Education Hub helped me get accepted to my dream university. Their support made the process easy and clear."

"My name is Sarah MUKAMANA and I am from Mugombwa refugee camp. Before I discovered Faustin’s Education Hub, I had almost lost hope of continuing my studies. I grew up in Mugombwa Refugee Camp, and for several years I missed many scholarship opportunities because I did not know where to find correct information or how to prepare strong applications. Sometimes I felt discouraged seeing others move forward while I stayed behind. After joining Faustin’s Education Hub, everything changed. I received guidance on scholarship searches, application writing, and confidence during the process. With their support, I successfully applied and was admitted to the University of South Africa. Today, I feel proud and motivated because my dream of higher education finally became real."

"Life in Kiziba Refugee Camp was not easy, especially when it came to education opportunities. For many years, I applied for scholarships without success because I lacked proper guidance and information. There were moments when I thought my university dream would never happen. Later, I learned about Faustin’s Education Hub, and it became a turning point in my life. The team helped me improve my applications, understand scholarship requirements, and stay motivated throughout the journey. Because of their support, I earned a scholarship opportunity at Makerere University. My story is proof that with the right guidance and determination, refugee students can also achieve great success."
